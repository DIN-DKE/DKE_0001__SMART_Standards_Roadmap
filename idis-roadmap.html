IDiS Roadmap

The following chart shows a timeline of completed, ongoing and planned working groups and publications that are part of the Initiative on Digital Standards (IDiS) by DIN & DKE. In the chart, working groups are mapped to a phase in the value creation process of digital standards (see whitepaper 1), with a special section for AI-related groups. The table below the chart provides further details.
